About

Beyond traditional backend work, I'm deeply curious about the intersection of machine learning, AI, and software engineering, and I enjoy exploring how intelligent systems can be applied in practical, impactful ways. I'm constantly learning, whether that's through building projects, experimenting with new tools, or diving deeper into complex technical concepts

Outside of coding, I enjoy sharing what I learn through blogging, and unwinding with classic films from the 20th century

Feel free to explore my projects and blog posts to get a glimpse of my work and interests. If you'd like to connect or collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out!
